Datsun Go+ Preview

Datsun GO+ MPV made its Indian debut at the 2014 Indian Auto Expo and shares platform with GO hatchback, but seems proportionally bigger and more spacious. Dimensionally, it measures 3995mm in length, 1635mm in width and 1490mm in height and has a wheelbase of 2450mm. With the third row of seats, which are made only for kids, the car offers a boot space of 48-litres, but it can be increased up to 347-litres by folding the third row seats. Though the vehicle has a decent ground clearance of 170mm, 13-inch tyres still feel undersized. In its segment, the MPV competes against the Maruti Ertiga, Mahindra Quanto and Honda Mobilio.

The affordable MPV priced in the vicinity of Rs 3.85 lakh and Rs 4.77 lakh (ex-showroom, Delhi) caters to the customers with large families looking for a low budget vehicle. The seven-seat configuration inside offers comfortable space in the first and second rows but the third row is meant only for kids. Under the hood, it gets the 1.2 litre, petrol engine missing out on the diesel unit and transmission duties are carried over by the five-speed manual gearbox.

Datsun GO Plus Exterior 

Deriving design cues from the GO hatchback, it gets an identical front profile and side facet. With the addition of the third row, the sub-compact MPV sports a different rear profile as compared to its hatchback sibling 

Datsun GO Plus Interior

Wheelbase measuring 2450mm implies comfortable space for front and rear passengers, although third row is cramped and offers room for kids. Boot space is confined at 48 litres, which can be further expanded to 347 litres by folding the third row seats. 

Datsun GO Plus Engine Specifications & Transmission

Datsun has used the same 1.2 litre petrol engine which does duty on the GO hatchback. The engine makes maximum power of 67bhp and 104Nm of top torque. A five-speed manual transmission is paired to the engine directing power to front wheels. 

Datsun GO Plus Performance & Mileage

Powered by the 1.2 litre petrol engine Datsun Go+ is claimed to deliver ARAI rated fuel economy of 20.62km/l, which is impressive. 

Datsun GO Plus Accessories & Infotainment

It gets a mobile docking station with amplifier, mobile holder, Aux-in, USB port and speakers on both sides. 

Datsun GO Plus Safety & Security

Driver airbag and manual central locking are available only on the top-spec variant. Other features such as 3 point ELR front seat belts, second row seat belts, third row seat belts, child lock and engine immobilser are standard on all trims.  

Datsun GO Plus Comfort & Convenience

Comfort features packed inside are drive computer, gear shift guide, follow me headlamps, remote fuel lid opener, accessory socket, heater & blower, air conditioner with filter, remote tailgate opener, front power windows and speed sensitive electric power steering. 

Datsun GO Plus Wheels & Tyres

The sub-compact MPV has good ground clearance, yet the 13 inch tyres appear undersized as against the body mass. 

Datsun GO Plus Competition 

The low priced MPV does not have a direct competitor but it rivals models including Maruti Suzuki Ertiga and Honda Mobilio.
